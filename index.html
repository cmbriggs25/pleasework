About Me

I am Caleb Briggs, an 18-year-old freshman at Brigham Young University, where I am pursuing a degree in marketing. With a deep sense of purpose, I am currently serving a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, reflecting my commitment to spreading faith and positivity. I'm not only a dedicated student but also a thriving social media influencer, with 177,000 followers on TikTok and 32,600 on Instagram, where I share my beliefs and lifestyle. In addition to my studies, I am an entrepreneur, currently working on launching my own merchandise line, Briggs Standard Apparel, which is centered on faith and self-expression. My brand's slogan, "Jesus Christ for Life" (JC4L), embodies my commitment to living a life of purpose, strength, and service. My passion for marketing and entrepreneurial endeavors is evident in my approach to blending faith and fashion, with my merchandise line standing as a statement of faith in everyday life. Outside of my academic and entrepreneurial pursuits, I enjoy music as part of an acapella group, further showcasing my creativity and collaborative spirit. With a strong foundation in both faith and ambition, I am building a bright future focused on serving others, empowering those around me, and living out my values in all aspects of my life.
